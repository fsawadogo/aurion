## Three live-probe bugs surfaced + fixed (PR #217)

Running the probe with `provider_override=anthropic` and `provider_override=openai` to verify the fallback chain revealed:

1. **`provider_override` query-string silently ignored.** Declared as `Form(default=None)` — only multipart body fields. Operators using `?provider_override=...` got Gemini regardless. **Fix:** swapped to `Query(default=None)`.
2. **ffmpeg NOT installed in production ECS container.** Anthropic + OpenAI clip-to-still fallback shells out to `ffmpeg`; production failed with `"ffmpeg binary not found on PATH"`. **The fallback chain was broken in production until this PR.** **Fix:** added `ffmpeg` to `backend/Dockerfile` apt-get + verified via `RUN ffmpeg -version`.
3. **Stale `_PROVIDER_MODEL_ID` constant.** Probe reported `claude-sonnet-4-5` but `anthropic.py:_MODEL = "claude-sonnet-4-6"`. Cosmetic but DRY-violating. **Fix:** probe imports each provider's `_MODEL` constant; single source of truth.

After #217 deploys to dev, re-running the fallback probe should show `provider_used: "anthropic"` with `degraded_to_frame: true` (the midpoint-still fallback path) — that's the proof gap #7 is closed.
